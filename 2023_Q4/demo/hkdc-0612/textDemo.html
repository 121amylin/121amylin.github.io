建議分段

HK Printing Group, Ltd. is more than a printing company- We are printing and beyond!

Since Caxton Chang opened the doors to HK Printing in 1975, adapting to meet the growing demands of our customers and the marketplace is what has set us apart.

Over the years, we’ve developed a true “one-stop shop”. Our team of graphic designers creates original concepts that can be applied to printed products and services; our production staff then working with state-of-the-art digital, large format and commercial printing equipment to produce the quality printed products that we’re known for.
